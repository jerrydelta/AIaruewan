低糖/净碳水饮食规划应用（日中食库+糖质目标，英文本地化）

Low-carb / net-carb diet planner with JP/CN food DB and 糖質 targets, EN-localized

💡 痛点

Low-carb / 糖質制限 (carb-restriction) dieting is huge in Japan and China but served mainly by generic calorie apps in the West. Chinese apps (轻食计划, 魔胴健康, 柠檬轻断食) target 生酮/低糖 specifically; Japanese 糖質制限 is a long-standing trend with dedicated books/methods but few dedicated apps. Gap: an English low-carb planner that mimics JP/CN specificity (net-carb focus, 糖質 targets, JP food DB) for Western keto/low-carb users.
